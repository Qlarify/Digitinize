The Problem

Why most marketing automation programs underperform.

A year in, most of those platforms are running at a fraction of their capability — sequences never built, scoring never configured, attribution never connected to CRM.

Tools are bought before strategy is defined. Lead scoring is set up before anyone knows what a qualified lead actually looks like in closed-won data. Email sequences get built without a clear lifecycle map. Fix the strategy first; the tool is the easy part.

FAQ

Questions we get most often.

Which platforms do you work with?

HubSpot most often. Marketo, Salesforce Marketing Cloud, and Customer.io for specific cases. We pick based on your stack and team — not the platform we're best paid to recommend.

Do you migrate platforms?

Yes — typically from generic ESPs (Mailchimp, ConvertKit, etc.) to HubSpot or Marketo. We've also done HubSpot-to-Marketo for enterprise stage clients.

Will you do the email writing?

Yes — all lifecycle and nurture content is produced in-house. Your team reviews and approves; you don't write.

How is this different from your demand gen service?

Demand gen brings buyers into the system. Automation accelerates them through it. Most clients run both — and the two services compound.

What's the typical timeline to a full automation buildout?

60–90 days to first stable state. ~6 months to full optimization with attribution and lead scoring calibrated to actual closed-won outcomes.
