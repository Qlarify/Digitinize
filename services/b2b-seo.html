01 / B2B SEO & Content

B2B SEO built around 9-month buying cycles.

Most B2B SEO programs are optimized for traffic. Yours should be optimized for pipeline. We build SEO around how B2B buyers actually search — over months, across multiple sessions, with research-stage queries that look nothing like the commercial queries you eventually want to win.

The Problem

Why most B2B SEO programs underperform.

Generalist SEO playbooks were built for high-volume, low-consideration searches. B2B doesn't work that way. Your highest-value commercial keyword may have 90 searches a month — and ranking #1 for it may produce more pipeline than 50,000 monthly visits to a "what is X" blog post.

Most agencies optimize for traffic because traffic is easy to report. We optimize for the 200-search-a-month commercial terms your buyers use when they're ready to evaluate vendors — and the long-tail informational queries that bring them in 9 months earlier.

What we do

The four pillars of a working B2B SEO program.

⚙️

Technical SEO foundation

Crawl, index, schema, and Core Web Vitals audit and remediation. The infrastructure that lets Google rank you at the moment your buyers are searching — and stops invisible drag from killing campaigns that should work.

🗺️

Topic cluster architecture

Topic clusters mapped to your ICP, deal size, and 6–9 month buying cycle. Every cluster has a commercial anchor and informational supports — and a clear conversion path back to your service or product page.

✍️

Editorial production

Four to eight in-depth pieces per month, founder-bylined where appropriate. Each piece carries a strong point of view, internal links to relevant clusters, and a clear next step for readers who are ready to engage.

📊

Content inventory & retirement

Quarterly content inventory: winners get expanded, losers get killed, gaps get filled. Most B2B sites have 80% dead content dragging crawl budget. We fix that.

FAQ

Questions we get most often.

How long until we see results from B2B SEO?

Material traffic shifts at 4–6 months. Pipeline impact at 8–12. We won't promise faster — anyone who does is either lying or about to publish 60 thin posts a month that Google will ignore.

Do you only work with companies that already have content?

No. Greenfield SEO is often easier than recovering a poorly-built site. We've taken several clients from zero indexed pages to category-leading organic positions inside 18 months.

Will you publish under our team's bylines or yours?

Yours. The byline strategy is part of authority positioning, and the credit belongs to your operators. We work from founder and SME interviews and edit toward your voice.

How do you measure SEO impact?

Search Console clicks and impressions for traffic, but pipeline attribution — first-touch and assisted — for impact. We build the attribution into your CRM during onboarding so the reporting reflects revenue, not vanity metrics.

What's the typical scope?

Six articles per month, quarterly technical audit, monthly internal linking pass, and topic strategy reviewed every 90 days. Larger programs add link earning, digital PR, and content refresh cycles.
