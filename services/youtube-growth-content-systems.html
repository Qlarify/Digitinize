Services / YouTube Growth

YouTube growth — strategy plus production, under one roof.

Most YouTube channels stall because the team is fragmented — a creator handling the script, an editor handling the cut, a freelancer doing thumbnails, and no one looking at retention. Growth happens when strategy, production, packaging, distribution, and analytics live inside one team. We run YouTube as a system for founders, creators, and brands serious about long-form.

The problem

Why most B2B and founder YouTube channels fail.

The intent is there. The system isn't. Three patterns show up across every stalled channel we audit.

🎯

No channel thesis

Random topics, untested formats, no editorial slot. The channel reads as "everything I think about" — and the algorithm can't categorise the audience.

📦

Packaging treated as afterthought

The thumbnail is designed in the last hour. The title is written from the script, not from the audience search. CTR plateaus, the channel never breaks out.

📈

Retention never read

Views tracked, retention curves ignored. The intro that's losing 40% of viewers in the first 30 seconds never gets diagnosed — and every new video repeats the mistake.

🔀

Long-form and Shorts run separately

Two production motions, two teams, no shared insight. Shorts that should funnel to long-form go nowhere, and long-form audiences never see the channel's Shorts.

Our model

The 5-pillar growth model.

Five pillars run in parallel. The team running pillar one is the team running pillar five — no hand-offs, no diluted feedback loop.

🧭

1 — Strategy

Channel thesis, audience definition, content slate, format playbook, and a 90-day editorial map. The map that everything else hangs on.

🎬

2 — Production

Studio + field production calibrated to the format. Cinema or prosumer gear depending on channel stage. Multi-cam for interview formats.

📦

3 — Packaging

Thumbnail design and title strategy treated as production work. Three thumbnail variants per video, title A/B tested where the channel supports it.

📤

4 — Distribution

Publishing cadence, Shorts-to-long-form funnel, community-tab calendar, end-screen architecture, and playlist strategy.

📊

5 — Analytics

Weekly internal reads, monthly client review. CTR, AVD, retention curves, traffic source mix, audience retention vs absolute retention. Data that drives the next slate.

🔄

Feedback loop

Every video informs the next. Hooks, intros, packaging, and topic selection refined month-over-month against the actual channel data — not industry templates.

FAQ

Questions we get most often.

How much does a YouTube channel audit cost?

A standalone channel audit runs ₹50K–₹1.5L depending on scope. Audit-only engagements cover positioning, packaging analysis, retention diagnostics, and a 90-day growth plan. Audits are credited toward a retainer if you continue.

What's the typical monthly long-form output?

Two to eight long-form pieces per month depending on retainer tier and video length. Founder/creator retainers typically run 4 long-form pieces a month plus 30+ Shorts.

Is thumbnail design included?

Yes. Packaging — thumbnail and title — is treated as production work, not an afterthought. Every long-form gets 3 thumbnail variants with A/B testing options.

How often do you review analytics?

Weekly internal reads, monthly client review. We track CTR, AVD, retention curves, and traffic-source mix — and recalibrate the content slate against what's actually growing.

Do you work with multi-channel brands?

Yes. Multi-channel engagements — founder channel plus brand channel, or two creator channels — are scoped together. Cross-channel insights compound.

How do you split long-form vs Shorts?

Long-form drives subscribers and watch-time monetisation; Shorts drive top-funnel discovery. The split is calibrated to channel stage — heavy Shorts for early growth, heavy long-form once the audience compounds.
