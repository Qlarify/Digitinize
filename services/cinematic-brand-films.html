Services / Cinematic Brand Films

Brand films, directed.

A corporate video documents a company. A brand film moves a buyer. The difference isn't budget — it's craft, treatment, and a director who has done the work to find the story before the camera arrives. We make director-led cinematic brand films for premium brands, ambitious businesses, and founders who need a single piece of film that stands on the brand for years.

The difference

What separates a brand film from a corporate video.

Most "brand films" in the market are corporate videos with a soundtrack. They explain the company. A brand film does something harder — it makes the viewer feel something specific about the brand and remember it. The craft difference is in five places.

📖

Story architecture, not script

A brand film starts with an arc — origin, tension, decision, stakes, resolution. The script comes later. Corporate videos start with a deck and end with a voice-over.

🎬

A director, not a coordinator

Every shot, every cut, every line of dialogue runs through a single creative voice. Account-managed productions diffuse the craft across three reviewers — and you can feel it.

💡

Cinema-grade craft

Cinema cameras, motivated light, considered sound design, original or premium-licensed music. The viewer can't articulate the difference — they just stop scrolling.

⏳

Built to last

A brand film should live on the home page for 12–24 months. Corporate videos age in three. We design the film to compound — not to expire with the campaign.

Our model

Director-led from treatment to grade.

One director assigned to your project. Same person who pitches the treatment is in the edit suite. The producer manages logistics; the director manages the film. No hand-offs, no diluted intent.

🎯

Brand & story discovery

Workshops with founders and brand leads. The single sentence the film has to prove, the audience it has to move, and the unfair story only your brand can tell.

📝

Director's treatment

The film on paper — tone, structure, scenes, music direction, references. Approved before pre-production begins.

🎥

Full production scale

Cinema cameras (Alexa, RED, FX9), anamorphic and spherical lenses, gimbal, drone, jib, lighting truck — gear scoped to the treatment, not the budget line.

🌍

Multi-location capability

India end-to-end. International shoots via partner crews in select cities. Permits, logistics, and travel managed by the production team.

🎼

Music & sound design

Original composition or premium licensed tracks with full commercial clearance. Sound design layered as craft, not as fix.

🎨

Colour & finish

DaVinci-graded, calibrated to the brand world. Final deliveries in master quality, broadcast, web, and social aspect ratios.

Process

An eight-week production rhythm.

WEEK 1–2

Strategy & treatment

Brand discovery, audience definition, story-arc selection. The director writes the treatment; the brand lead signs off.

WEEK 3–4

Pre-production

Scripting, storyboards, casting, location scouting, shot list, lighting plan, call sheet. The shoot is built before it is shot.

WEEK 5

Production

One to four shoot days with a director-led crew. Daily review of dailies, talent direction, and pickup management.

WEEK 6–7

Offline edit & director review

First cut, director cut, two structured rounds of client review. Music direction and sound design layered in.

WEEK 8

Online, grade, deliver

Colour grade, online finish, final sound mix, deliverable masters and platform-native cuts. Project archive handed over.

FAQ

Questions we get most often.

How much does a cinematic brand film cost?

A director-led cinematic brand film typically runs ₹8L–₹60L. The range reflects scale — single-location founder-led films at the lower end, multi-location campaign films with talent, music licensing, and post-grade work at the upper end.

What's the typical timeline?

Eight to twelve weeks end-to-end. Two to three weeks of strategy and treatment, two weeks of pre-production, one to four shoot days, and four to five weeks of post-production with director review cycles.

Is there a director attached to every project?

Yes. Every cinematic brand film is director-led from treatment through final grade. We don't run agency-style account hand-offs — the director who scoped the film is in the edit suite.

Can you shoot across multiple locations?

Yes. Multi-city, multi-country, multi-location shoots are within scope. Logistics, permits, and crew are managed end-to-end — the client sees one production lead, not a vendor stack.

Do you produce in multiple languages?

Yes. Native-language scripting, voice direction, and subtitling across Hindi, English, regional Indian languages, and major export languages. Single shoot, multi-language deliveries.

What's your reshoot policy?

Reshoots are scoped if the original brief changes, talent direction shifts post-shoot, or new scenes are required. We don't reshoot for taste-level disagreement on already-approved treatment — that's what the treatment sign-off is for.

How is music licensing handled?

Two paths — licensed library tracks (cleared globally, commercial use included), or original composition. Composer engagements typically add ₹50K–₹3L depending on instrumentation and brief.
