02 / LinkedIn & Paid Media

LinkedIn and paid media for B2B revenue teams.

LinkedIn ads work for B2B. They also burn money faster than any other B2B channel when run by teams that treat them like Meta campaigns. The difference is in how you target accounts, structure creative around deal size, and connect spend to pipeline — not impressions.

Book a diagnostic → See all services
The Problem

Why most LinkedIn programs underperform.

Three patterns repeat. First, audiences are built from job-title targeting alone, ignoring account fit — so you reach the right titles at the wrong companies. Second, creative is built for awareness when the budget can only afford conversion — so the spend never converts. Third, attribution stops at lead-form fills, so the team optimizes for cheap leads that never close.

Each is fixable. Together they explain why most LinkedIn budgets in India produce no measurable pipeline.

What we do

A LinkedIn program that compounds.

🎯

Account targeting from your CRM

Audiences built from your closed-won data, not LinkedIn's defaults. We layer firmographics, intent signals, and named-account lists to spend only on accounts that actually look like buyers.

🎨

Deal-size-aware creative

Thought leadership at the top of funnel, deal-size-aware messaging at the bottom. A creative system that adapts to where each account is in the buying cycle — not one ad set for everyone.

🔁

Retargeting flywheel

Paid, organic LinkedIn, email, and SEO reinforcing each other. The audience you build through paid gets nurtured organically; the audience you build organically gets accelerated through paid.

📈

Pipeline attribution from day one

Reporting tied to your CRM pipeline definition — not the agency's. We build the attribution before the first campaign goes live, so month-three numbers mean something.

FAQ

Questions we get most often.

What's a reasonable monthly LinkedIn budget for B2B?

Below ₹4L/month, it's hard to generate enough data to optimize. Below ₹2L, don't bother — invest in organic LinkedIn instead. Above ₹6L the program starts compounding.

Do you also run Google Ads?

Yes, for branded and high-intent commercial queries. We don't run broad keyword campaigns — they almost never work for B2B in India.

How long before we see pipeline?

Depends on your sales cycle, offer clarity, and audience size — but early signals (conversations, inbound DMs, demo requests) typically appear in the first quarter. Compounding pipeline — where retargeting and ABM start reinforcing each other — builds over 4–6 months.

Will you work with our existing in-house team?

Yes, usually as the senior layer above an internal executor. We're happy to upskill in-house teams, not replace them.

What about Meta and X?

We almost never recommend Meta for B2B. X situationally for certain founder-led brands where the founder is already building an audience there.
