Industries / Real Estate

Real estate films that sell the lifestyle, not just the square footage.

Most property videos do one of two things — fly a drone around the building, or walk a steadicam through empty rooms. Neither sells. Buyers commit to a project because they can see themselves living there before the brochure ever lands. We direct cinematic real estate films for developers, luxury properties, and project launches — engineered to move the buyer, not just document the build.

The problem

Why most property videos fail.

A category drowning in interchangeable drone clips and templated walkthroughs. Five patterns that show up in almost every developer's content library — and none of them sell.

🎥

Walkthroughs that look identical across competitors

Same gimbal pass, same empty kitchen, same dusk-lit balcony. The footage is technically clean but it never tells the buyer why this project — not the one next door.

🛸

Drone footage with no narrative arc

Aerial reveals shot beautifully and then stitched into a montage that goes nowhere. Drone is a tool, not a story — and most films treat it as both.

📸

Marketing teams stuck repurposing photos

Static renders crossfading on a soundtrack. The medium is wrong — moving images sell properties; sliding stills feel like a placeholder while the real film is being made.

📊

Investor pitch videos that don't convey premium feel

Decks read aloud over stock B-roll. Capital partners notice production value because it signals operational discipline — and most fundraise reels signal the opposite.

🏡

Launch films that miss the lifestyle promise

The film describes the amenities. The buyer wanted to feel the morning, the weekend, the dinner with friends. Lifestyle isn't a checklist — it's a tone you direct, not list.

What we produce

Six real estate film formats — each with a job to do.

Every format below is engineered for a specific moment in the buyer journey — launch announcement, walk-in conversion, broker enablement, investor pitch, paid social. We don't shoot generic; we shoot to function.

🎬

Project Launch Films

The hero film. Lifestyle-led, director-led, built to anchor a launch campaign across PR, paid, and sales-floor moments. 90 seconds to three minutes.

🚁

Drone Cinematography

Aerial work that earns its place in the cut — reveals, transitions, context. DGCA-certified pilots and a story brief, not just a sky shot.

🚪

Walkthrough Films

Premium walkthroughs with motivated camera, considered light, and a buyer's-eye narrative. Built to convert at the sales lounge.

🌅

Lifestyle Brand Films

The morning routine, the rooftop dinner, the school run. Films that show what living here actually looks like — calibrated to the buyer persona.

💼

Sales Pitch Films

Long-form (5–7 minute) films built for the sales-floor walk-through — pricing, configurations, value engineering, on camera and on brand.

📈

Investor Presentation Films

Fundraise-grade films for institutional partners — track record, project economics, leadership vision. Production quality that signals seriousness.

How we work

Story architecture, then shoot.

Every project starts with story before storyboard. We've directed brand films for luxury developers, integrated township projects, and city-defining mixed-use launches — and the pattern that holds across all of them is the same: the script is built before the gear list.

01

Discovery & story architecture

Buyer persona, project DNA, competing developers, and the single sentence the film has to prove. Two weeks of strategy before any camera moves.

02

Pre-production

Site recce, talent casting, location calendar, drone permissions, shot list, lighting plan, and director's treatment signed off before the call sheet goes out.

03

Production

One to three shoot days with a director-led crew. Cinema cameras, anamorphic where appropriate, gimbal, drone, and a producer keeping the schedule clean.

04

Post-production

Offline edit, director review, online finish, colour grade, sound design, music licensing. Two structured review rounds — not a feedback free-for-all.

05

Delivery & derivatives

Master film plus paid-social cuts (15s, 30s, 60s), broker reels, sales-lounge loops, and aspect-ratio variants — built from the master, not reshot.

FAQ

Questions we get most often.

How much does a real estate launch film cost in India?

A standalone launch film typically runs ₹6L–₹20L depending on scale, locations, and crew. Multi-asset launch bundles — master film, drone reels, walkthrough cuts, and ad creative — sit in the ₹15L–₹40L range. We scope precisely after a creative brief and site recce.

Do you handle drone permissions and licensing?

Yes. We work with DGCA-certified drone pilots and handle no-fly zone clearances, site permissions, and the documentation required for residential, commercial, and pre-launch sites.

How long does a luxury property film take to produce?

Six to ten weeks end-to-end. Two weeks of pre-production and story architecture, one to three shoot days, three to four weeks of post-production with grade, sound, and director review cycles.

Can you shoot in pre-launch (under-construction) sites?

Yes — and we plan around it. We use 3D walkthroughs, CGI integration, drone passes, and lifestyle footage shot elsewhere to deliver a finished film well before handover. Site safety and PPE are non-negotiable.

What's the difference between a walkthrough video and a brand film?

A walkthrough documents the property. A brand film sells the lifestyle, the location, and the buyer's future inside it. Walkthroughs serve sales floors; brand films drive launches, paid media, and PR.

Do you do paid-social short-form cuts from the master film?

Yes. Every master film is built knowing it will be cut into 15s, 30s, and 60s variants for Meta, YouTube, and broker channels — aspect ratios, hook frames, and pacing planned from pre-pro.
