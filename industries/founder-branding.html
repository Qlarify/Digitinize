The problem

Why most founder content doesn't scale.

The pattern shows up across tech CEOs, healthcare leaders, consulting founders, and creator-economy entrepreneurs. The intent is there. The system isn't.

📅

Inconsistent posting

Three posts in a week, then nothing for a month. Algorithms reward consistency, audiences reward depth — and one-off bursts deliver neither.

🎙️

Awkward on-camera presence

Founders who are sharp in a meeting freeze on camera. Without coaching, prompts, and a comfortable shoot environment, the footage never matches the person.

🗺️

No content strategy

Random thoughts, recycled industry takes, hot quotes from podcasts. Without a thesis, the content reads as noise — and a year in, the audience hasn't moved.

🎬

Generic talking-head videos

Flat lighting, single static frame, no B-roll. The visuals signal "side project" — and the founder's authority is undercut before the first sentence lands.

🔁

No system for repurposing

One shoot, one video. The 30+ derivatives — clips, carousels, audiograms, quote cards — that should live inside every studio day never get built.

How we work

One day in. Thirty days out.

01

Founder discovery

A working session with the founder — thesis, audience, prior content, voice, what's worked, what hasn't. The input that everything else is built on.

02

90-day editorial map

Pillar themes, content arcs, sample headlines, and the platform mix. Approved before the first studio day so production is on rails.

03

Monthly studio day

A four-to-six hour shoot in our Bangalore studio or your office. Multi-cam, teleprompter, interview-led — calibrated to how you actually communicate.

04

Post-production & packaging

Editor-strategist pairing per channel. Long-form first, then short-form derivatives, then carousels and quote cards from the same source material.

05

Distribution & review

Weekly drops, monthly analytics, quarterly thesis review. Content recalibrated based on what's actually moving the needle — not vanity metrics.

FAQ

Questions we get most often.

How much does a founder content retainer cost?

Founder content retainers typically run ₹1.5L to ₹6L per month depending on volume and platform mix. Entry tier covers one monthly studio day plus LinkedIn output; full tier covers multi-platform output across LinkedIn, YouTube, and Reels with distribution support.

How many videos do you produce per month?

One studio day produces 4–8 long-form pieces and 30–60 short-form derivatives. Depending on retainer tier, monthly output ranges from 12 to 60+ assets, all cut from a single shoot.

Do you ghost-write the scripts?

Yes. Our scripting team works in the founder's voice — informed by interviews, internal docs, and recorded conversations. The founder reviews and refines; they don't write from scratch.

Do you handle distribution and posting?

Yes, on full-tier retainers. We manage the publishing calendar, post to LinkedIn and YouTube, and run light engagement support. We don't run paid amplification — that sits with media partners.

What if I'm camera-shy?

Most founders we work with started camera-shy. We use teleprompter, interview-style prompts, and a small studio crew to get founders camera-ready inside 60–90 days. The shoot day is built around how you actually communicate.

Can you film at our office vs in a studio?

Both. We run a Bangalore studio for controlled-light interview days and travel to client offices for field shoots, leadership meetings, and team-context footage that fills out a content month.

Do you cover both LinkedIn and YouTube?

Yes. LinkedIn long-form and short-form, YouTube long-form and Shorts, plus Reels and X video where relevant. Aspect ratios, hooks, and pacing planned per platform from the script stage.
