The problem

Three patterns that drain eCommerce video budgets.

Every D2C marketer we talk to is fighting the same fires. The shoot was expensive. The output was thin. The performance is plateauing. And the founder's voice never shows up in the content stack.

📉

Performance ad fatigue

Three creatives in rotation, all built around the same hook. CPMs creep, CTR collapses, and the media buyer is asking for variants the production team can't deliver.

💸

Expensive shoots producing 3 ads instead of 30

A ₹4L shoot delivering three deliverables. The footage existed for thirty — but no one storyboarded the derivatives before the camera rolled.

📦

No system for SKU launches

Every new SKU triggers a one-off shoot, a one-off agency brief, and a one-off creative cycle. Cost-per-launch never falls; speed-to-market never improves.

👤

Founder content disconnected from product content

The brand has a founder story. The performance team has a product feed. The two never meet on camera — and the brand reads as commodity in feed.

🎨

Lifestyle and performance produced in silos

Two crews, two shoot days, two creative directions. Brand and performance built apart — when the highest-converting feed treats them as one.

How we work

A production system, not a campaign.

Most D2C brands buy shoots. We sell systems. The first engagement is usually a master product film plus a monthly performance creative retainer — then founder content and lifestyle social drops layered in.

01

Creative diagnostic

Audit of current creative, ad account performance, SKU roadmap, and brand stack. The input that decides the next shoot's hook library.

02

Hook architecture & storyboard

Hooks engineered for the audience, the offer, and the platform. Storyboards built knowing 30+ variants will be cut from the master.

03

Shoot day(s)

SKU-bundled shoot days. Product, lifestyle, talent, and founder shots captured in one logistical sweep — not three.

04

Variant production

Editor-strategist pairing per platform. Hero cut, lifestyle cuts, performance variants, PDP cuts — all sequenced for the launch calendar.

05

Test, read, refresh

Monthly variant refresh tied to ad-account data. Winning hooks scaled, losing hooks retired, new variants cut from existing footage.

FAQ

Questions we get most often.

What's the cost per SKU for eCommerce video?

Cost per SKU depends on shoot bundling. A single SKU shoot ranges ₹50K–₹2L. Bundled SKU days (8–15 products per shoot) drop the per-SKU cost to ₹15K–₹40K including hero film, lifestyle cuts, and platform variants.

How many ad variants can you produce from one shoot?

A single one-day shoot typically produces 20–40 ad variants across hooks, lengths, and aspect ratios. Performance-creative retainers scale that to 50+ variants per month with a structured testing pipeline.

Are your formats performance-tested?

Yes. We design ad creatives against a tested hook library — hooks, formats, and pacing that have hit positive ROAS for similar AOV ranges. Hooks are refreshed quarterly based on what's converting in market.

Do you script and creative-direct, or just shoot?

Both. Our scope includes concept, hook architecture, scripting, casting, and creative direction. We don't just execute a shot list — we build the creative against the offer and the audience.

Can you produce UGC-style at scale?

Yes. We run a paid-creator network for UGC-style production with brand QA — premium quality without the agency feel. Output ranges from 10 to 50 UGC-style assets per month under retainer.

Do you handle multi-platform aspect ratios?

Yes. Every shoot is planned to deliver 9:16, 1:1, and 16:9 native cuts — Meta, YouTube, TikTok, Amazon, and PDP. Framing, hook position, and pacing optimised per platform from the storyboard stage.
