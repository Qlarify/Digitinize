How We Work

From the first call to
compound growth.

Four phases. One north star: qualified pipeline. We move quickly because we've done this before — and we've learned that the diagnostic phase is where most agencies lose the engagement before it starts.

The process

Four phases. One north star:
profitable growth.

01
Weeks 1–2

Deep Audit

Before we touch your accounts or write a single brief, we learn your business. Ad accounts, email performance, unit economics, customer data, category dynamics: all of it. We leave no stone unturned because the best growth strategy is built on what's actually true about your brand, not what we assume.

Full paid account audit Lifecycle health check Creative performance analysis CAC:LTV model Category competitive audit Growth bottleneck map
02
Week 3

Growth Blueprint

The audit informs a 90-day growth blueprint: a prioritised plan that tells you exactly what we're building, why, in what order, and what result we expect from each initiative. No vague strategy decks. Every recommendation tied to a number.

90-day roadmap Priority stack-ranking Expected outcomes by initiative KPI framework Resource requirements
03
Weeks 4–8

Build & Launch

We build the systems (campaigns, flows, creative frameworks, dashboards) and get them live. Not in theory. In your accounts. Generating data. The first 60 days are about building infrastructure and gathering signal.

Campaign restructure Lifecycle flows live Creative testing launched Attribution model deployed Weekly reporting live
04
Week 9 → ongoing

Compound & Scale

This is where the work compounds. Weekly iteration loops: what's working gets scaled, what isn't gets killed or rethought. Over months, CAC tends to drop, LTV tends to rise, and revenue becomes more predictable.

Weekly optimisation syncs Creative refresh cycles Budget scaling framework Quarterly strategy review LTV cohort tracking
What's different

The things we commit to
from day one

👤

No account manager layer

The people on the intro call are the people who do the work. You have direct access to the strategist, the media buyer, the creative lead — all of them.

📞

Proactive, not reactive

We don't wait for you to ask what's happening. We bring problems, opportunities, and recommendations to you before you notice them.

📊

Revenue-tied reporting only

We don't report impressions or reach. We report CAC, ROAS, repeat rate, and revenue. If it doesn't connect to money, it's not in the dashboard.

🏗️

We build things that last

Every system we build is documented, transferable, and yours. If you ever leave, you keep the infrastructure, not a black box.

🎯

Small roster, full focus

We work with a small number of brands at a time. That's a feature, not a constraint. Your brand gets genuine attention.

🔁

Long-term mindset

We're not here for a 90-day sprint. We're here for the compounding. Our average client relationship is 18+ months.

Our commitment

What you can hold us to

  • A growth blueprint within 3 weeks of starting
  • Live campaigns and flows within 6 weeks
  • Weekly reporting, every single week
  • Access to the actual experts, not intermediaries
  • Honest assessment if something isn't working

What we need from you

We work best with founders and operators who are close to their numbers, open to honest feedback, and in it for the long game. If you want someone to tell you what you want to hear, we're not your team.
