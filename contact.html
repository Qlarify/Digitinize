FAQ

Questions we get often

What's the minimum engagement size?
We typically work with brands doing ₹10L+ a month in revenue. Below that, the economics of a senior growth team don't work for either side. If you're earlier, our playbooks are a better starting point.
How quickly can you start?
Once we've agreed to work together, we typically start the audit within 5–7 business days. Our onboarding is lightweight by design — we've done it 17 times.
Do you work with brands outside Bangalore?
Yes. We work with D2C brands across India. Most of our engagements are fully remote — we've built our process to work asynchronously without losing quality.
Do you require long-term contracts?
Our retainers run month-to-month after an initial 3-month commitment. We don't believe in locking clients in — we believe in earning the relationship every month.
Can you just do one thing — like paid or email — rather than everything?
Yes. Many engagements start with one or two service pillars. We're upfront about the interdependencies — sometimes fixing paid without fixing lifecycle is like filling a leaky bucket.
What categories do you specialise in?
D2C only, across fashion, beauty, wellness, home & living, and food & beverage. We don't work with B2B, SaaS, or brick-and-mortar retail. D2C requires a different muscle — we've built it.
