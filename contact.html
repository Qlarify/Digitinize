FAQ

Questions we get often

What types of B2B companies do you work with?
SaaS, IT services, consulting, enterprise software, professional services, healthcare & healthtech, manufacturing, and technology companies. If your buyers are businesses, your sales cycle is complex, and revenue matters more than traffic. We're built for you. We don't work with D2C or consumer brands.
What's the minimum engagement?
We work on 90-day initial sprints or monthly retainers. We don't take on one-off projects outside of standalone audits, because pipeline doesn't come from single deliverables. Our typical client stays 18 months.
How fast can we start?
7–10 business days from signed agreement to kick-off. Onboarding runs in parallel with access setup so we don't lose time.
Do you work with companies outside India?
Yes. We work with B2B companies across India, Southeast Asia, the Middle East, and internationally. Core team is Bangalore-based. The work travels.
How is this different from hiring an agency?
Three things: we embed rather than service (you get a team member, not a vendor), we measure against pipeline rather than deliverables (we own the number, not just the output), and we keep a deliberately small client roster so every engagement gets senior-level attention. No account managers. No junior execution teams. No handoffs.
What do you need from us to make this work?
Access to your leadership team, your sales data, and your CRM. Willingness to be honest about what's working and what isn't. And a view of marketing as a revenue function, not a support function. The best results come from clients who want a partner, not a vendor.
