## 6 · AI Search Optimization

### 6.1 The new search reality
ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity now drive a meaningful share of high-intent research. Their citation logic favours:
- **Entity-first content** (clearly defined services, locations, people)
- **Conversational FAQ depth** (answers in question form, with full sentences)
- **Schema completeness** (LocalBusiness, Service, Person, FAQPage, BreadcrumbList, VideoObject)
- **NLP-friendly structure** (semantic HTML, descriptive H-tags, no keyword-stuffed pages)
- **Cited references** (industry data, awards, named clients with permission)

### 6.2 Semantic keyword architecture
For every page, build a **3-layer semantic map**:

```
PRIMARY ENTITY  →  cinematic brand film
PROPERTIES     →  director, runtime, format, production scale, deliverables
RELATIONSHIPS  →  used-for-industry, used-for-channel, complemented-by, alternative-to
```

Mark each in schema. Use Wikipedia entities where they exist (`sameAs` schema property).

### 6.3 Entity-based SEO targets

Define and own these entities on the site (one page each):
- Digitinize Creative (Organization)
- Cinematic Brand Film (Service)
- Founder Branding (Service)
- Short-Form Content System (Service)
- Commercial Video Production (Service)
- Recruitment Video Production (Service)
- Each city as `Place` entity with coordinates
- Founder / Director as `Person` entity with `sameAs` LinkedIn

### 6.4 Conversational SEO — FAQ depth strategy
Every cornerstone page should answer **at least 7 questions** matching common search phrasings:
- "How much does ____ cost in India?"
- "How long does it take to ____?"
- "What's the difference between ____ and ____?"
- "Can you ____ for ____?"
- "Do I need ____ if I already have ____?"
- "When should we hire ____ vs ____?"
- "How do you measure ____?"

Each answer 60–120 words, full sentences, no bullet lists inside FAQ schema (AI parsers prefer prose).

### 6.5 AI-citation maximisers
- **Comparison pages** (e.g. "Brand film vs corporate video", "Director-led vs agency-led production") — AI answers love comparison content
- **Definitive guides** with named methodology (e.g. "The Digitinize Creative 8-Week Brand Film Process")

### 10.5 Anti-patterns to avoid

- **Sound on demand** — every embedded film with a clean unmute icon, never default-on
