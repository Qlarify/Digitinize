The founding story

Ten years inside B2B. We know how pipeline gets built.

Digitinize was founded by Zeeshan Soudagar in 2014 — when systematic demand generation was still rare among Indian B2B companies. Not because he spotted a trend. Because he'd watched too many excellent B2B companies — great products, real value, smart teams — struggle to grow because they were buying generic marketing from generalist agencies.

The problem wasn't the tactics. It was the structure. Agencies were optimised for retainers, not results. For deliverables, not decisions. For account managers, not operators.

So we built the kind of B2B growth partner we wished existed: embedded, accountable, senior, and honest about what actually moves pipeline — versus what creates the appearance of activity.

What we believe

The principles that shape
how we work

01

Pipeline is how we keep score.

Impressions, reach, engagement: we don't optimise for these. Every decision we make is traced back to whether it puts more revenue on your P&L.

02

Honest beats comfortable

If your funnel is broken, we'll tell you. If your creative strategy is wrong, we'll say so. The best growth partner is the one willing to deliver bad news fast.

03

Systems outlast campaigns

A great campaign lasts a quarter. A great system lasts years. We build the infrastructure: the flows, the testing frameworks, the attribution models. All of it compounds.

04

B2B is a different discipline

MQL, SQL, ACV, sales cycle length, pipeline velocity — these are B2B-specific levers. We've spent 10 years mastering them. We don't accept clients outside B2B.

05

Small roster, total focus

We keep a deliberately small roster, and turn away work when taking it would dilute focus for existing clients. You won't be lost in a crowd of accounts.

06

We build what we'd want to own

Every system, playbook, and framework we build for your brand is transferable. If you ever leave (we'll work hard to prevent it), you take your IP with you.
