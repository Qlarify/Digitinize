Built by operators,

So we built the kind of growth partner we wished we had. Embedded, accountable, senior, honest.

Our track record

Ten years building
D2C growth machines

2014
Founded in Bangalore

Started with two D2C fashion brands. Built the first paid acquisition framework that tied ad spend directly to repurchase behaviour — not just first-order ROAS.

2016
Lifecycle marketing practice launched

Saw that most D2C brands were leaving 40%+ of revenue on the table post-purchase. Built the lifecycle practice before "retention marketing" was a common D2C term.

What we believe

The principles that shape
how we work

01

Revenue is the only metric that matters

Impressions, reach, engagement — we don't optimise for these. Every decision we make is traced back to whether it puts more revenue on your P&L.

02

Honest beats comfortable

If your funnel is broken, we'll tell you. If your creative strategy is wrong, we'll say so. The best growth partner is the one willing to deliver bad news fast.

03

Systems outlast campaigns

A great campaign lasts a quarter. A great system lasts years. We build the infrastructure — the flows, the testing frameworks, the attribution models — that compounds.

04

D2C is a different discipline

CAC, LTV, payback periods, repeat purchase architecture — these are D2C-specific levers. We've spent 10 years mastering them. We don't accept clients outside D2C.

05

Small roster, total focus

We will always turn away business to protect the focus of our existing clients. You will never be one of 50 accounts. You will always be one of a handful.

06

We build what we'd want to own

Every system, playbook, and framework we build for your brand is transferable. If you ever leave — which we'll work hard to prevent — you take your IP with you.
